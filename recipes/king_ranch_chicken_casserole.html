King Ranch Chicken Casserole

Delicious and yummy Chicken casserole!

Describing the taste of this delicious dish is pointless, you will have to trust us on this one and just get to making it as soon as possible!

Making this dish will not take a lot of your time, the only problem you will face is having the self control to finish making the dish without having your saliva all over. please wear a mouth guard.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables: 1 onion, 2 bell pepper, vegetable oil
  • Canned Soup
    • condensed cream of chicken soup
    • condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Chilli pepper
  • Chicken broth
  • Sour cream
  • Spices ad seasonings of your choice:Cumin, oregano, chipotle chile, anything you want
  • Cooked Chicken
  • Cheese, again, any type you prefer. We recommened shredded cheddar cheese
  • Tortillas

Ok, so now that we have all the materials, lets get into the steps!

  1. Saute the onion and bell peppers
  2. stir the sauteed vegetables with canned soups, diced tomatoes, chicken broth, sour cream and spices. make sure everything is well mixed
  3. spread a couple of tablespoons of the sauce on the bottom of a baking dish, then top the sauce with half the chicken, then spread half the sauce over the chicken and top with 1/3rd of cheese. arrange layers of tortillas over the cheese. add remaining chicken then top with remaining sauce. top with remaining cheese and tortillas in couple more layers.
  4. BAKE!

    on 350 degrees F untill bubbling, set to broil for few minutes to get golden color
